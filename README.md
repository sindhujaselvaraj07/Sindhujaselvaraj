### üöÄ About Me
I work across digital accessibility and quality engineering, bringing more than ten years of experience in building products that everyone can use. My background covers accessibility audits, inclusive design, manual and automated Accessibility testing, and and close collaboration with cross functional teams across banking, healthcare, government, and tech.

I possess commendable knowledge in Accessibility audit & remediation in both Web accessibility (Desktop & Mobile). I am skilled in Accessibility automation tools such as aXe, ARC, Parashu, Lighthouse, WAVE evaluation tool and well versed with different assistive technologies such as NVDA, Voiceover, Talkback, JAWS, Narrator and ChromeVox. 

I have also done POC on Accessibility Automation testing using Deque's dev tool and axe-core npm package using ESLint, and Playwright. Over the years, I have supported designers with accessible wireframes like Figma, guided developers on ARIA and semantic HTML, reviewed UI builds, and integrated automated accessibility checks into CI pipelines. I enjoy solving practical accessibility problems and helping teams adopt better practices.

I recently worked as an Accessibility Intern at the Waiheke Resource Trust in Auckland, where I supported audits, verified fixes,  and helped the team improve their website for real users. Before that, I worked as a Staff Engineer at Altimetrik where I drove accessibility compliance, reviewed designs, mentored teams, and ensured products met Global Accessibility standards including WCAG, ADA, AODA, Section 508 standards, the European Accessibility Act, and EN 301 549. 

Accessibility is not just my area of expertise, it's my passion. I strive to make the digital world more inclusive and believe that every user deserves equal access to technology.

If you or your team would like support in assessing the accessibility of your website, mobile applications, or guide teams toward inclusive design,  I would be happy to help.
